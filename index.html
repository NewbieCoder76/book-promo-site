About the Book

Every once in a generation, out of a vast continent emerges a writer whose engaging and purposeful use of words effortlessly moves the needle. The book, Lion Among Sheep, is a culture-shifting tool which engages all the senses of the reader. Lion Among Sheep is a workbook which will set the reader on a journey of responding to the silent inner-tugging which has often crossed their mind. Often what we fear the most is to become who we truly are. And as a result, we lull ourselves into false mental prisons where we spend most of our lives fitting in. These are invisible mental cages which castrate and shave us - causing us to doubt who we truly are, and as a result, live below our true capabilities. Lion Among Sheep artfully and transcendentally blows each of these cages open. It then sets the reader on a courage-filled path of crossing those nameless limitations, guided through timeless principles. If you have any connection to Africa. If your loved ones or your roots extend to Africa. If you have ever considered making your impact on the continent or out of Africa. You are encountering Lion Among Sheep at just the right time. Now Africa, your Creator knows the thoughts that He thinks toward you. These are thoughts of peace and not of evil, to give you a future and hope. Your time has come.

“Faith is not the absence of doubt, but the courage to move forward.”

About the Author

Dumisani Sondlo is an experienced leader, mentor, public speaker and entrepreneur. In his work life, he has traversed such diverse areas as information and communication technology, digital skills, multilateral negotiations, academia and multimedia. Dumisani has served as national lead in the development of his country’s artificial intelligence (AI) policy. In his public speaking and writings, he seamlessly weaves the timeless art of storytelling, rooted in his upbringing on the continent of Africa. He impactfully mixes a keen sense of strategic, professional, corporate governance, and solution-orientation - with a unique ability to identify patterns of mindsets, habits and cultural impediments. Possessing multiple awards, Dumisani is also a devoted follower of Jesus Christ, a husband and father.
